About Us

The Panchayat Proposal Management System is designed to streamline the process of proposal submission, review, and approval within the local governance framework. It aims to enhance transparency, efficiency, and accountability in managing development projects proposed by the Panchayats.

Purpose

The system facilitates the seamless flow of proposals from Panchayat members to the Block Development Officer (BDO) and finally to the District Commissioner (DC) for approval. It ensures that all proposals are reviewed and processed in a timely manner, with clear communication at each step.

Key Features

  • Secure login for Panchayat members, BDOs, and DCs
  • Proposal submission form for Panchayat members
  • Dashboard for tracking proposal statuses
  • Review and comment functionality for BDOs
  • Approval or rejection by DCs
  • Email and in-app notifications for status updates
  • Historical tracking of all proposals

User Roles

  • Panchayat Members: Submit new proposals and track their status.
  • Block Development Officer (BDO): Review and comment on proposals, provide recommendations.
  • District Commissioner (DC): Approve or reject proposals based on BDO's recommendations.

Workflow

  1. Panchayat members submit proposals through the proposal submission form.
  2. The BDO reviews the submitted proposals, adds comments, revised estimates, and scrutiny.
  3. After BDO's review, proposals are forwarded to the DC for final approval or rejection.
  4. The DC reviews the proposals and makes the final decision, which is communicated back to the Panchayat members and BDO.
  5. Notifications are sent at each stage to keep all stakeholders informed.

Benefits

  • Improved transparency in the proposal management process
  • Efficient handling and tracking of proposals
  • Timely updates and notifications for all stakeholders
  • Enhanced accountability and record-keeping
